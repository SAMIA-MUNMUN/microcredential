What is a mosque?

A mosque is a place where Muslims worship God (ALLAH). It can be an open space rather than buildings. It is a central gathering place for Muslims, both for worship and faith and also contains teaching and community spaces. Muslims have to pray 5 times a day, they can perform at home or in a Mosque.Though its not mandatory, its recommmendable to pray in the Mosque as by going to the Mosque,the entire society can meet at a place and can know each other, and also that is how a society can bond strongly. When visiting a mosque, one should dress modestly. Entering a mosque, one can hear “Assalamu Alaikum” which means “peace be upon you.” The correct reply is “Wa alaikum-as-salam” which means “peace be upon you too”. In the Mosque, you will hear Adhan (which is called 5 times a day to call muslims for prayer), which is very pleasant and soothing to the heart.

Description of these Mosques:

Assalam Center:

It's a beautiful Sunni Mosque located in Boca Raton,Florida.


Islamic Centre of Washington DC:

It is an institution that is located in Washington DC and stands ready to help anyone who may reveal a desire to embrace Islam and serves as a source of guidance for Muslims.


East Plano Islamic Centre:

EPIC is a non-profit organization that has been formed exclusively for educational, religious, and social purposes. This beautiful Mosque is located in Texas.


Al-Farooq Masjid:

It's the largest Mosque in the Southeastern United States. It is located in Atlanta, Georgia.
